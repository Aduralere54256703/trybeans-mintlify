Unlock the power of personalized customer engagement with Beans Emails, a built-in email marketing solution within the Beans loyalty platform. This integrated tool empowers businesses to connect directly with customers through targeted, tailored communications. By seamlessly merging Beans Emails with the loyalty program, merchants can effortlessly promote rewards, updates, and loyalty opportunities via email, engaging a more informed, enthusiastic, and engaged customer base.

# Setup

1. From the Beans admin page, go to integration.

2. Locate Beans email from the list of available integrations.

3. Click on the **connect** button and proceed as directed.

# Emails Log

<Frame>
  <img src="/assets/images/integrations/beansemail/email-log.webp" alt="email-log" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

The email log section provides a full record of all emails sent through Beans, along with in-depth insights into email interactions such as:

1. Date and time of email sending.

2. The event that triggers the email.

3. Email content and recipient.

4. Status of email delivery (opened, delivered, etc.).
